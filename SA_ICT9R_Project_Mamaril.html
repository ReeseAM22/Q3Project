Don't set your house on fire by:
Supervise Your Cooking

The fires from your stoves can easily enlarge and spread. Prevent any distractions that can lead your cooking unsupervised. If required to leave the kitchen, either turn off your stove or ask someone else to keep a eye out.
Proper Storage

Flammable substances and products should be properly hidden away from nearby places that fire or heat could be used, like fireplaces, kitchens, etc.

Avoid Overloading Your Sockets

Only plug a number of electrical plugs that is required in socket. Prevent overloading your sockets as it can overheat and result to a fire.
Don't Place Wirings Under Carpets

Do not place electrical wirings that run across under your rugs or carpets as it may rub against the soft mat and cause a fire through heat friction.

Buy a Fire Extinguisher

Having this can lessen the chances of fire spreading. It can lessen anxiousness knowing you have such an item that can save lives your lives and many others. Place this somewhere easy to get to.
